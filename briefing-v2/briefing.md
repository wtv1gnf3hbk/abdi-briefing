Israeli forces killed a Syrian child in overnight strikes on Lebanon and separately assaulted two children during an incursion into Syria's Quneitra countryside, [according to Enab Baladi](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/two-killed-including-syrian-child-in-israeli-strikes-on-lebanon/). The Lebanon strikes killed two people total, while [Syria TV reports](https://www.syria.tv/%D9%82%D9%88%D8%A7%D8%AA-%D8%A7%D9%84%D8%A7%D8%AD%D8%AA%D9%84%D8%A7%D9%84-%D8%AA%D8%AA%D9%88%D8%BA%D9%84-%D9%81%D9%8A-%D8%B1%D9%8A%D9%81-%D8%A7%D9%84%D9%82%D9%86%D9%8A%D8%B7%D8%B1%D8%A9-%D9%88%D8%AA%D8%B9%D8%AA%D8%AF%D9%8A-%D8%B9%D9%84%D9%89-%D8%B7%D9%81%D9%84%D9%8A%D9%86) that Israeli troops entered the Quneitra countryside Friday evening and attacked two shepherd boys. Both incidents underscore escalating tensions along Syria's borders as Israeli operations extend beyond Lebanon into Syrian territory.

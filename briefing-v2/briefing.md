US Ambassador Mike Huckabee's suggestion that Israel has biblical rights to much of the region, including parts of Jordan and Syria, has drawn sharp condemnation from Arab governments and raised questions about Washington's diplomatic approach in the Middle East. Egypt, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia denounced his remarks as "absurd and provocative," [according to Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/21/absurd-and-provocative-huckabee-faces-firestorm-for-israel-border-stance?traffic_source=rss), with Jordan calling the comments a direct threat to regional stability. The controversy comes amid ongoing security challenges across the region, including Islamic State activity in Syria that continues to threaten the post-Assad transition.

Iran launched a massive retaliatory missile barrage across the region following US-Israeli strikes that killed Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei, marking the end of his 37-year rule and triggering the most significant escalation in Middle East tensions in decades. The Iranian retaliation, involving dozens of ballistic missiles fired at multiple targets, has caused civilian casualties from falling debris in Syria, forced flight disruptions across the Levant, and prompted regional authorities to issue emergency warnings to citizens. Tehran confirmed Khamenei died in the overnight strikes, according to the [Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/trump-says-evil-khamenei-is-dead-israeli-official-says-his-body-has-been-found/), while Lebanese authorities work to [reassure citizens](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/lebanon-authorities-reassure-citizens) gripped by panic as the escalation threatens regional stability.

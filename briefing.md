The Islamic State has claimed two separate attacks in Syria while launching a broader propaganda campaign to rally supporters across the country, according to [Enab Baladi](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/islamic-state-claims-two-attacks-after-rallying-supporters-in-syria/). The group claimed responsibility for an attack on government forces in Deir Ezzor and another operation, marking what appears to be an [escalation against](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/islamic-state-escalates-against-syrian-government/) Syria's new leadership. The timing coincides with ongoing tensions at al-Hol camp, where 1,200 Iraqi nationals are refusing to leave despite UN-coordinated repatriation efforts for other detainees.

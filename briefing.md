**Lebanon**

• Lebanon's Economy Ministry [intensified market inspections](https://nna-leb.gov.lb/en/news/short/152214) and warned against unjustified price hikes, per NNA. The move comes as authorities try to control inflation during Ramadan when consumer spending typically rises.

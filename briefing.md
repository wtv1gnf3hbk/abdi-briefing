The US and Iran concluded talks in Vienna with both sides claiming progress toward a nuclear agreement, though officials provided few specifics about whether core differences can be resolved to avoid potential conflict, according to [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/26/us-iran-talks-conclude-claims-progress-few-details?traffic_source=rss). Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi said the two countries have "moved closer to reaching an agreement," while technical discussions are set to continue next week, [Times of Israel](https://www.timesofisrael.com/us-iran-said-to-agree-to-further-talks-next-week-as-mediator-claims-progress/) reports. The diplomatic push comes as tensions remain high over Iran's nuclear program and regional activities that directly affect Syria and Lebanon.

**Syria**

• Syrian government and Suwayda factions completed a [prisoner exchange](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/suwayda-detainee-swap-between-government-and-national-guard/), according to Enab Baladi, following negotiations over detained members of both sides.

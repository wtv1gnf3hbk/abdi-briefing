Israeli airstrikes hit Beirut overnight after Hezbollah fired rockets at northern Israel in apparent retaliation for the broader Iran conflict, forcing tens of thousands of Lebanese civilians to flee the capital, [according to Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/video/newsfeed/2026/3/2/al-jazeera-reports-from-beirut-as-residents-flee-following-israeli-strikes?traffic_source=rss).

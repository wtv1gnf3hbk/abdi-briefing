Massive explosions and fires lit up Tehran overnight as US and Israeli forces launched coordinated strikes on Iranian military targets, according to [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/video/newsfeed/2026/3/7/massive-explosions-fires-from-us-israeli-strikes-on-tehran?traffic_source=rss).

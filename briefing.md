The Syrian Network for Human Rights has documented the execution of 21 detainees in Kobani, according to [Enab Baladi](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/syrian-network-for-human-rights-documents-execution-of-21-detainees-in-kobani/), marking a significant escalation in northeast Syria where tensions between the SDF and Damascus have been mounting. The killings come as the Islamic State has claimed responsibility for two separate attacks in Syria, [per Enab Baladi](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/islamic-state-claims-two-attacks-after-rallying-supporters-in-syria/), raising questions about security coordination between Syrian government forces and Kurdish-led authorities.

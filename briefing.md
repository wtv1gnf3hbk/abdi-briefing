US Ambassador Mike Huckabee sparked a diplomatic firestorm across the Middle East after suggesting Israel has biblical rights to vast swaths of regional territory, according to [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/21/absurd-and-provocative-huckabee-faces-firestorm-for-israel-border-stance?traffic_source=rss). Egypt, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia denounced the remarks as "absurd and provocative," [Times of Israel reports](https://www.timesofisrael.com/arab-and-muslim-states-fume-after-huckabee-says-israel-has-biblical-right-to-much-of-mideast/). The comments come as regional powers are already on edge over Israeli actions in Gaza and the West Bank. Jordan, which hosts millions of Palestinian refugees and maintains a peace treaty with Israel, faces particular pressure from the ambassador's suggestion that Israeli territorial claims could extend well beyond current borders.

**Syria**

• Syrian leader Ahmed al-Sharaa issued a [general amnesty](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/on-the-eve-of-ramadan-al-sharaa-issues-a-general-amnesty/) ahead of Ramadan, according to Enab Baladi, marking another gesture toward national reconciliation in the post-Assad era.

• The Syrian Interior Ministry established new [citizenship procedures](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/the-syrian-interior-ministry-sets-procedures-for-syrian-citizenship-applications-for-kurdish-residents/) for Kurdish residents seeking Syrian nationality, per Enab Baladi, as Damascus works to integrate previously autonomous areas.

• A Syrian government delegation arrived at [Qamishli Airport](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/syrian-government-delegation-at-qamishli-airport-ahead-of-reopening/) ahead of its planned reopening, Enab Baladi reports, signaling further integration between Damascus and the formerly SDF-controlled northeast.

• The SDF released [51 detainees](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/sdf-releases-51-detainees-in-al-hasakah-calls-for-a-phase-of-forgiveness-and-rebuilding/) in al-Hasakah and called for a "phase of forgiveness and rebuilding," according to Enab Baladi.

• Canada eased Assad-era [Syria sanctions](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/canada-eases-sanctions/) while targeting "new perpetrators," Enab Baladi reports, reflecting international efforts to recalibrate policies for post-Assad Syria.

**Lebanon**

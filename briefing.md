The Trump administration asked the US Supreme Court to allow deportation of Syrian migrants by stripping them of Temporary Protected Status, arguing Syria is no longer unsafe, [according to Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/26/us-government-asks-supreme-court-to-allow-deportation-of-syrian-migrants?traffic_source=rss). The move comes as thousands of Syrians who fled the Assad regime now face potential return to a country still rebuilding from civil war. The case represents Trump's broader effort to reverse immigration protections established under previous administrations, with potential implications for tens of thousands of Syrian refugees across the US.

**Syria**

US Ambassador to Israel Mike Huckabee sparked a diplomatic firestorm across the Middle East after suggesting Israel has a biblical right to much of the region, according to [Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2026/2/21/absurd-and-provocative-huckabee-faces-firestorm-for-israel-border-stance?traffic_source=rss). Egypt, Jordan, and Saudi Arabia denounced the remarks as "absurd and provocative," with officials saying the comments undermine regional stability. The controversy comes as Syria's new government works to establish diplomatic ties with neighboring Arab states and as Iran reportedly increases its control over Hezbollah operations. Huckabee's statements also coincided with Islamic State claiming a [renewed offensive](https://news.google.com/rss/articles/CBMiyAFBVV95cUxPTTRvazJHMFNQWDF4UkNnLTJ0VGw1VVNEVndaZ3NtX0M0b25ZM0dfSUtUREhWaUY0X1M0V0Y2eFpNZ0JuSlhfRDhIeE4xTUxCVjFpTDhkWDBqWnZUQkRGMG5MX2J6X0hSdXpkU0tXUlZzLW1pYk5OWnFnWEhrZ2p2VTlLQ2RnU0JabFZLdC1lUUNnRWdYY2pUS0lQQ2tEZWwyTy1nNEI2eFlFQ29PMFFzZE8tRFpFOTA) against Syrian forces, announcing what Reuters calls a "new phase" of operations.

**Syria**

• Syrian leader Ahmed al-Sharaa issued a [general amnesty](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/on-the-eve-of-ramadan-al-sharaa-issues-a-general-amnesty/) ahead of Ramadan, marking another gesture toward national reconciliation in the post-Assad era, Enab Baladi reports.

• The Syrian Interior Ministry published [new procedures](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/the-syrian-interior-ministry-sets-procedures-for-syrian-citizenship-applications-for-kurdish-residents/) for Kurdish residents to apply for Syrian citizenship, per Enab Baladi, as Damascus works to integrate marginalized communities.

• Syrian government officials arrived at [Qamishli Airport](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/syrian-government-delegation-at-qamishli-airport-ahead-of-reopening/) ahead of its planned reopening, according to Enab Baladi, in another sign of growing cooperation between Damascus and SDF-controlled areas.

• The Syrian Democratic Forces [released 51 detainees](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/sdf-releases-51-detainees-in-al-hasakah-calls-for-a-phase-of-forgiveness-and-rebuilding/) in al-Hasakah and called for a "phase of forgiveness and rebuilding," Enab Baladi reports.

• Canada announced it was [easing Assad-era sanctions](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/canada-eases-sanctions/) on Syria while targeting "new perpetrators," per Enab Baladi, as Western governments recalibrate their approach to the new Damascus government.

**Lebanon**

• The Lebanese Hospital Syndicate issued an [urgent statement](https://olnnews.com/181415/) condemning threats made against Salah Ghandour Hospital in the south via leaflets, OLN News (Lebanon) reports, calling the targeting of medical facilities a violation of international law.

**Broader Levant**

• Iran's Revolutionary Guards are reportedly [running Hezbollah operations](https://www.timesofisrael.com/report-irgc-running-hezbollah-preparing-it-for-war-with-israel-if-us-strikes-iran/) and preparing the group for potential war with Israel if the US strikes Iran, according to Times of Israel, signaling increased Iranian control over the Lebanese militia.

• Israeli forces [detained two Syrian men](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/https-wp-me-p7cv3y-oft/) in the Yarmouk Basin area of Daraa province, Enab Baladi reports, amid continued Israeli operations in Syrian territory.

• The UN coordinated the [return of 191 Iraqis](https://english.enabbaladi.net/archives/2026/02/un-coordinates-return-of-191-iraqis-from-al-hol-camp/) from Syria's al-Hol camp, per Enab Baladi, as international efforts continue to repatriate foreign nationals from detention facilities.

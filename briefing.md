Israeli strikes killed at least nine people, including five children, in the Lebanese village of Arki overnight, as the escalating Iran-Israel conflict spreads across the region, [according to Al Jazeera](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/liveblog/2026/3/13/iran-war-live-trump-says-war-going-well-as-gulf-under-wave-of-attacks?traffic_source=rss). The attack on Arki was part of a broader wave of Israeli bombardment that killed dozens more across Lebanon, while Iranian missiles struck residential areas in northern Israel, wounding at least one woman and dozens of others. Netanyahu claimed Israeli strikes had killed Iranian nuclear scientists, [Times of Israel reports](https://www.timesofisrael.com/netanyahu-says-israeli-strikes-killed-iranian-nuclear-scientists/), as oil prices stayed above $100 per barrel due to Iran's control over the Strait of Hormuz. The widening conflict has drawn President Trump's assessment that the war is going "well" for Israel.
